From navigating as a lost tourist to embracing new perspectives and capturing moments through photography, my journey has been about exploring and understanding new places. This blog began as a fun attempt to share the dream I've been living, and it's been fulfilling to recount these experiences during my travels and spare moments.

I owe so much to appamma and Nishchith for shaping me through this journey, for their endless support and for keeping me grounded in the midst of chaos. A heartfelt thanks to Padmapriya, my constant motivator, and the spark behind this blog. And of course, a big thank you to Nihal and Nikitha for the unforgettable adventures and for always looking out for me!

As the last few days set in, I enjoyed my time at the university, interacted with my colleagues, and bid farewells with the hope of meeting again someday. Over the weekend, Nihal brought a bunch of premium Swiss chocolates that we had planned to buy. After some shopping and exploring the city of Jena for the first time, I packed my bags. We also explored the local beauties of Erfurt and Naumberg, and were deeply fascinated by their blissfulness. Then, it was time to leave! After a quick catch-up at the university, Nihal and I headed out, and as I bid goodbye to him, I carried on to Dubai and finally home.

This journey isn't just mine; it's ours together. Thanks for being a part of it! Here's to more adventures ahead! 😊

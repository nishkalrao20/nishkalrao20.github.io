***We don't see things as they are, we see them as we are***

Life sometimes feels like a dream you never want to wake up from. It's incredible how our consciousness, formed from stardust, allows us to feel, explore, answer, understand, build, love, trust, believe, and grow. This summer, I embarked on a journey of soul-searching that would change the course of my life forever.

Here is my humble attempt at capturing the beautiful moments and reminiscing about my journey through the European paradise. From the magnificent treasures of Germany 🇩🇪, exploring the heavenly Swiss landscapes 🇨🇭, to the charm of France 🇫🇷, the fairytale city of Prague 🇨🇿, serene Austria 🇦🇹, the friendly Dutch 🇳🇱, and finally, the enchanting Italy 🇮🇹.

During my three months in Germany, I decided to explore the majestic Europe with my Schengen visa. Every weekend, I ventured out on student trips and solo adventures, honing my photography skills and admiring the breathtaking beauty around me. From waterfalls to terrains to mountains, I experienced it all. With each week of my internship, I embraced cooking, traveled over the weekends, explored new places, and met fascinating people, staying in everything from student dorms to impressive hotels. It was a complete package!

I'm your host, Nishkal, and welcome aboard.
